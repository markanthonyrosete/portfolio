Services: Banking and development-focused financial services
Founded: 1947

B. Nature of the Agency

The Development Bank of the Philippines (DBP) is a government-owned development financial institution established in 1947 to support national development and economic growth. It provides financing and banking services to key sectors such as infrastructure, public services, environmental projects, and business development.

C. Mission & Vision

Mission

To support infrastructure development, responsible entrepreneurship, efficient social services, and the protection of the environment. It also works to raise the competitiveness of the economy for sustainable growth while promoting high standards of good governance.

Vision

By 2040, the Development Bank of the Philippines aims to be a world-class infrastructure and development financial institution, and a proven catalyst for a sustainably progressive and prosperous Philippines.
